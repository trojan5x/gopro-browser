Connect your GoPro in seconds

This app runs entirely in your browser. A small local proxy bridges your GoPro's USB connection to bypass browser security policies. Setup takes under 30 seconds.

1
Run the local proxy server
Open your terminal, copy this command, paste it, and press Enter:
curl -s https://raw.githubusercontent.com/trojan5x/gopro-browser/main/gopro-proxy.py | python3 copy
2
Plug in GoPro and power it on
Connect the camera to your Mac or Windows PC via USB-C, then press the physical power button on the GoPro.
