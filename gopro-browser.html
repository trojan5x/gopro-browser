GOPRO BROWSER
Connect your GoPro in seconds.
This app runs entirely in your browser — a small local proxy bridges your GoPro's USB connection. One-time setup, takes under 30 seconds.
1
Run this in your terminal
Requires Python 3 (pre-installed on Mac). No installs, no dependencies.
curl -s https://raw.githubusercontent.com/trojan5x/gopro-browser/main/gopro-proxy.py | python3copy
2
Plug in your GoPro via USB-C and press power
Works with GoPro Hero 10 and later. The camera's HTTP API activates automatically when powered on with USB connected.
Proxy not detected at localhost:8765. Make sure the terminal shows "Serving on → http://localhost:8765" then try again.
